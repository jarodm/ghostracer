GhostRunner Login

Ghostrunner allows you to track your progress against a goal and provides real-time status to help you make your target. Beat your previous personal record, attempt to match your friend's personal record, or set a new goal. Using GPS technology, the app tracks your distance and speed, then lets you know when to push that extra bit harder to make your goal. The post-run summary provides information on your progress as well as what you would need to do to close the gap.
